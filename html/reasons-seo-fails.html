5 reasons SEO fails

As an online marketer, you have read a lot of books, watch videos and even practice SEO to ensure high ranking and increase traffic, it is rather unfortunate that after investing time, money and effort in your SEO campaign it seems you are not seeing results, something could be wrong or there is something you are not doing right, here are some reasons SEO fails.


  • Outdated Tactics

    Google and other search engines are constantly updating their algorithm which leads to a change in SEO best practices, If you have been out of the game for a long time and back with the same old method you used and see result, you need to get updated along with the recent trends else you may not see your SEO effort being rewarding.

  • Poor Website design

    Simple and user-friendly web design decreases the bounce rate, you may be surprised that your website design affects how people interact with your site. If your design is such that the user can not freely navigate, not clear to the user, even if you have a valuable product, it will not convert the lead to a customer.

  • lack of analytics and goal tracking tools

    Analytics tools is used to measure the effectiveness of your website, it is absurd How marketers and website owners do not use analytics tools to track their SEO progress. It is known that SEO is a long term plan, any long term project should be monitored on a weekly or monthly basis so you see and understand the progress on a daily basis Your SEO strategy will definitely fail if you are not tracking the activities you only depend on your SEO company for data and result.

  • Narrow keyword choice

    A keyword is the foundation of every successful online business, It must match your website goals. If your keyword is too narrow and does not receive enough search volume you can not be seen, if your business is tied to one or two keywords it will affect your SEO.
